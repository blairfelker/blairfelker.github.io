D&D

I've played tabletop games for a while now, and I've started to keep track of all of my characters in no particular order.

Assume the system I've played these for is D&D 5e, unless stated otherwise.

Xavier

Campaign: ???

Race: Drow

Alignment: Lawful Evil

Class: Storm Sorcerer

My first character. Xavier lived in the underdark and had ties with the mafia. Being a Storm Sorcerer, he loved to use spells that dealt lightning and thunder damage. He especially loved "Ball Lightning," which was just Fireball, but lightning damage. After meeting the party, he agreed to work with them for a portion of the money. His last stand was in a volcano. After hitting a well-timed lightning bolt on two hags, the hags threw him into the lava below, causing him to perish.

Chance (Cheese) Ethorium

Campaign: World of Gods and Idols

Race: Human -> Tiefling

Alignment: Chaotic Neutral

Class: Alchemist Artificer

Chance was a gun dealer by trade who had an affinity for gourmet cheeses. After one of the Gods, Kryciert, saw him doing these trades, he was turned into a horribly mangled Tiefling. During his adventures with the party, he brewed a potion which allowed him to wish for one thing. He wished to return to a human. Unfortunately, the wish was faulty, and his form became even worse than before. The party inevitably faced against Kryciert, realizing that she cursed a lot more people than just Chance. Kryciert was able to release the curse on everyone else, except for Chance. This was due to the wish he had attempted to grant himself. He left the party, as he he failed.

Al Mite

Campaign: World of Gods and Idols.

Race: Half-Elf

Alignment: Lawful Evil

Class: Twilight Cleric

Al Mite came from a poor family and was born into indentured servitude to one of the party members, Concord. As a cleric, he attempts to find the good in anyone. He also seems to be obsessed with forgiveness, even forgiving Concord because he wasn't the one who enslaved him. This obsession also leads him to be a bit of a hypocrite. This hypocrisy was never explored, unfortunately, since the campaign was abandoned.

Hosbin Drawndecks

Campaign: Lizard King

Race: Human

Alignment: True Neutral

Class: Hexblade Warlock/College of Lore Bard

After his house burned to the ground, he needed power. What better way to get power than to have a goddess of death grant it to you. Now a servant of the raven queen, he was tasked with finding any remnants of necromancy and bringing them back 6 feet under. After witnessing a group getting annihilated by a Beholder, a new team assembled to take down the Lizard King, who was bringing back soldiers to conquer the world.

Angr Kinjh

Campaign: Curse of Strahd

Race: Half-Orc

Alignment: True Neutral

Class: Zealot Barbarian

From a mountain range north of Daggorford, his family if of warriors. After besting his brother to receive the family heirloom, The Greataxe of the Kinjh, he set off to "beat up some motherfuckers." Angr was eventually hired to kill werewolves in Barovia. After finding the rest of the party, who have similar goals, we set off to do our job. Angr was one of two remaining original party members at the end of the campaign due to his anger issues, making him a valuable asset in fights.

Arin Grin

Campaign: Adventures of Anbennar

Race: Human

Alignment: Neutral Good

Class: Open Hand Monk/Spore Druid

During the Lilac Wars, Arin barely escaped with his life as a child. He then learned martial arts to both relearn stress and defend myself. After traveling with the party, seeing adventurers come and go during his expeditions of the world, he realizes he has found new family.

Scout E. "Terrafall" aka "Rock Recker" Wasler

Campaign: Collateral

Race: Human

Alignment: Lawful Neutral

System: Mutants and Masterminds 3e

Like any other superhero comic, Scout's backstory is being made up on the fly. After having a radioactive wall fall onto him, he seeped into the earth and being granted power from "Mother Nature". With his Boston accent in tow, Scout joined Butcher's Boys simply because his father disowned him, and he was no longer a part of high society. He secretly wishes that he could go back, but he can't. This is particularly due to Mr. Waslers abusive nature, and his newfound fear of elevators. His powers require the natural earth to be around him.

Lang (Genesis) Larp

Campaign: Lost Chapter of Dugaia

Race: Human

Alignment: True Neutral

Class: Wildfire Druid

Before his mentor's death, Lang was an alchemist by trade. After drinking a supposed "potion of immortality," Lang truly began to believe he was immortal and became obsessed with gaining knowledge. Even though he wasn't very good at remembering things, at one point he became enamored with Druidic magic during his time in the Blighted Forest. After getting kicked out, he met the party to help them find the various artifacts to prevent the prophecy of Dugaia from being fulfilled.

Rid Lee

Campaign: Pirate Game

Race: Blue Purple Gem Dragonborn

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Class: Conquest Paladin

A general of the Space Pirates, Rid Lee was transported onto the kingdom of New Azuria. A sociopath by trade, he has no qualms with killing random people he deems weak and collects heads of strong opponents just for fun. While he was born a blue Dragonborn, he secretly wishes he was purple. To do this, he douses himself in the blood of his enemies. After gathering with the rest of the party to find the One Piece, he claimed his share of the loot. In his share of the loot was the Pirate Scepter, an all-powerful artifact that can shape reality. Unfortunately, he has no clue how to use it. Before he had a chance to use the pirate scepter, he fell in battle. A botched resurrection left his spirit destroyed and he left his dreams of reuniting with the Space Pirates. He had no longer recognized himself. If he were to come back one day, he would trek a path of vengeance.

Chopin Block

Campaign: Pirate Game

Race: Goliath

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Class: Wolf Totem Barbian

Chopin (pronounced Chopping) Block is a "hood gangster" who is "keeping the streets cold." While he is a moronic idiot who thinks he's a real gangster, he does possess a talent in combat. Able to bolster others while dual-wielding his Flame Tongue and Frost Brand, he helped the party obtain the Arctal Tear and defeat the demon of greed, Lucian Blythe.

Argentinalander (Messi)

Campaign: The Bloodbride Oneshot

Race: Human

Alignment: Chaotic Neutral

Class: Bladesinger Wizard

After the Old Testament Crew woke up in a fountain, they find the current Princess of the ruling kingdom, her maid, and her bodyguard in a conquered town. After teaming up with them, they realize the maid is actually an android. This fact is not supposed to be known by people outside of the monarchy, resulting in a combat between the 4 Landers and the Monarchy. While fighting the three of them, Argentinalander landed multiple blows by using Time Stop, but ultimately perished when he was hit by a well-timed Meteor Storm.

Thirti-on Thyrte

Campaign: The Great Dragon-Ball Oneshot

Race: Frieza Race

Alignment: Chaotic Good

Class: Champion Fighter

Not a big fan of the Government, he was locked up in prison because he didn't pay his taxes. After escaping prison, he found the party during a scuffle with a bunch of other idiots. During this adventure, the party met Nagukab, a being who is able to absorb the Dragon Balls. To combat him, the party first had to go to the Dragon Ball Factory. In order to obtain a set of Dragon Balls, at least one person in the party needed to be Pure of Heart. That one person was Thirti-on. After obtaining the Dragon Balls, the party was able to best Nagukab, even after Thirti-on needed to 1v1 a weakened version of him in the Tournament of Power. After this, the party disbanded.
