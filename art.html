3D Modeling

I was introduced to 3D Modeling in 9th grade when my engineering class had us use Inventor for a project. We needed to make a puzzle from cubes, then model it. I fell in love with this and while I currently use Fusion 360, I still enjoy creating things in Fusion.

Raspberry PI Case

While this isn't necessarily a case, it still looks cool when I put my Raspberry PI into it. It is based off of Tombstone from Battlebots. The case itself went over a couple iterations, and actually took me over a year to be happy with it. The whole project cost around 5 dollars, 1 for each print.
